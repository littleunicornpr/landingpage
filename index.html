An ECDA Certified & Approved Preschool

Little Unicorn is proud to be one of the leading ECDA certified preschools in Singapore. As an ECDA approved preschool, we meet the highest national standards for quality early childhood education and care — giving your preschool aged child the perfect start.

What ECDA Certification Means

Being a certified preschool by ECDA ensures that our programs, educators, and learning environments meet stringent guidelines to promote safe, effective, and age-appropriate education for young children.

Benefits for Preschool Aged Children
